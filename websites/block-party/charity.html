Charity

Each year we run a charity raffle. You can purchase a raffle ticket for $20. First, second, and third place prizes are $100, $75, $50 respectively. The remaining money will be donated to the following charities below.

Charities are randomly chosen by the residents of Fountain Green. Each year we randomly select 4 from their list. You may vote on your preferred charity. Each charity below will receive money proportional to the votes.

All block party attendees are able to vote.

Raffle numbers will be drawn after the last event of the day. We will post the winning numbers below, in case you weren't able to attend the drawing.
