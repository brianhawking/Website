Board Games/Card Games

10:00am - 11:00am
Bag Toss Tournament

Also known as Cornhole. Grab a friend or get paired with another player. Players take turns tossing bean bags at the hole on the opposite board until all bags are thrown. Points are tallied and then it's your partner's turn. Game continues until a team earns 21 points.

This is a tournament with the normal structured brackets. Once you lose, you're out.

Make sure you come cheer on your friends and neighbors.

12:30pm - 1:30pm
Water Balloon Toss

Grab a friend or get paired with another player. Each player will gently toss a water balloon to their partner. If the other player catches it successfully, the partners take a step backwards. Continue doing this until you're either the last team left or you break your water balloon. Once you break the balloon, the game's over for you.
